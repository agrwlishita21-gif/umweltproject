# Examples & Tutorials

Learn Umwelt through interactive examples that demonstrate multimodal-first data exploration. Unlike traditional visualization tools, Umwelt is designed for blind and low vision screen reader users to explore data through text and audio first, with visuals as an optional output for sharing with sighted colleagues.

## Multimodal-First Approach

Umwelt lets you:
- **Start with your data** and immediately hear patterns through sonification
- **Navigate rich text descriptions** that reveal structure and insights  
- **Add visuals when needed** for presentations or mixed audiences
- **Experience all modalities together** for complete understanding

You don't need to design a chart to explore your data - text and audio are first-class representations.

## Learning Philosophy

### 1. Data First, Not Charts First
- **Load your data** and understand its structure
- **Hear patterns immediately** through automatic sonification
- **Navigate text descriptions** to build mental models
- **Add visuals strategically** when needed

### 2. Screen Reader Excellence
- **Rich text descriptions** using Olli provide complete data access
- **Keyboard navigation** gives full control over exploration
- **Speech integration** coordinates with audio data playback
- **Multiple pathways** ensure you never hit accessibility dead ends

### 3. Visual When Useful
- **Communication tool** for mixed-vision teams
- **Verification method** for audio-discovered patterns
- **Presentation format** for broader audiences
- **Optional enhancement** rather than primary interface

## Tips for Screen Reader Users

### Start with What You Know
1. **Use your screen reader skills** - text navigation works like other web content
2. **Audio exploration** builds on natural pattern recognition
3. **Visual creation** uses the same logical mappings you've learned

### Exploration Strategy
- **Begin with data overview** through text descriptions
- **Listen to sonification** to hear overall patterns
- **Dive deep** with keyboard navigation for specific insights
- **Create visuals last** when you need to communicate findings

### Communication Benefits
- **Full data understanding** before creating presentations
- **Confident explanations** based on complete exploration
- **Strategic visual choices** informed by audio/text insights
- **Accessible presentations** that work for all audiences

## Why This Matters

Traditional accessible visualization tools assume sighted users are the primary audience, with accessibility as an afterthought. Umwelt flips this:

- **Screen reader users are the primary audience**
- **Text and audio are first-class data representations**
- **Visuals support communication needs** rather than dominating the experience
- **Complete data understanding** doesn't require visual interpretation
